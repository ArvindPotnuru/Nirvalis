### What's the Usual Norm?

**Early Stage (MVP)**: 
- Use Formspree or simple form handlers
- Focus on collecting emails quickly
- No complex automation needed

**Growth Stage**:
- Migrate to ConvertKit or Resend
- Add email sequences
- Implement automation

**Scale Stage**:
- Custom backend with Resend/SendGrid
- Full control and customization
- Integration with your app
